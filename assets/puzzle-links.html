Puzzle Links

Additional puzzles are linked to below for you to download at your leisure. These puzzles are not available for automatic download in Words With Crosses for one of several reasons:

  • The puzzles are only available by subscription;
  • The author or publisher has asked me not to include their puzzles for automatic download;
  • I've not yet written a downloader for the puzzles;
  • No new puzzles are being published; or
  • The puzzles are not organized or easily scrapable
